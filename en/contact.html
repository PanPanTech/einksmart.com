FAQ for partners and project teams.

These answers keep the offer grounded: clear scope, realistic support, and no speculative promises.

What is included in the core platform?

The public offer is a B2B platform direction: color E-Ink terminals, content packages, remote refresh planning, partner materials, and project support. Exact scope is confirmed per pilot.

Do you lock basic display flows behind hidden subscription walls?

No. Core display and project expectations should be clear before deployment. Optional content packages, custom work, or service plans are scoped separately.

How do you handle privacy and project metadata?

Project discussions focus on deployment and content metadata. Personal photos or sensitive customer data should not be sent through the public inquiry form.

Can you provide authorized content?

Yes, for selected projects. Authorized content can include artist approval, usage scope, Art ID metadata, and optional watermarking. We avoid speculative NFT-style positioning.

How are delivery and support handled?

We align landed cost, lead time, content scope, support contact, and pilot milestones before launch. Conservative delivery promises are preferred over overpromising.
