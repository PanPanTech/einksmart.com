Product

A color E-Ink story frame connected to a living AI world

MagiRealm combines color e-paper hardware, AI agents, story generation, and spatial display into a new B2B content terminal.

Four product layers

  • Color E-Ink frame: low-power, paper-like, always present.
  • AI world engine: autonomous characters create evolving events.
  • App management: character view, gallery, creation, slideshow, devices.
  • AI plush interface: emotional, conversational, high-frequency interaction.

V1.0 foundation

The initial product layer makes the frame demonstrable, usable, and deployable.

AI Creation

Text-to-image, image-to-image, style selection, candidates, favorites, and wallpaper push.

Smart Wallpaper

Generate scenes based on time, location, weather, holidays, and solar terms.

Gallery

Create albums, upload photos, collect images, set slideshows, and batch stylize.

MagiRealm experience layer

Character View

Daily Story Scene

Extract key story moments from the AI world and turn them into display art.

World Continuity
