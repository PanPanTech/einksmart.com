Technical FAQ

Why color E-Ink?

Color E-Ink is low-power, paper-like, and visually calm, making it suitable for long-term spatial display.

How does MagiRealm maintain story continuity?

The system records character memory, world events, relationship changes, and timeline summaries before generating scenes.

Can it support multi-device management?

The MVP website reserves the B2B deployment direction. Device groups, content push, status monitoring, and APIs can be added in the product platform.
